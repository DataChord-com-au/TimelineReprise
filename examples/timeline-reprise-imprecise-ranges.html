SIMILE Timeline Reprise: Imprecise Ranges

Regression example for a bug where collision handling correctly rerouted duration labels across multiple label tracks, but some tape-to-label sparklines lost their connection: retaining geometry from an earlier placement, continuing past a label, or terminating away from it. The events below overlap heavily and carry long labels, so they route across several label tracks (and some labels stick to the right viewport edge), which is exactly the situation that exposed the disconnect.

  • every sparkline should visibly touch its own label, in every label track
  • this holds after the initial layout, and continues to hold after resizing the window or panning the band
  • no sparkline should point at a different event's tape or label
